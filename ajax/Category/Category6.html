Magnatek provides a number of different X-ray sources for many different inspection tasks.

It could be low energy X-ray sources used where very small thickness- or density variations are attenuated and needs to be detected. Low energy-X-ray technology allows detailed analysis of both the packaging and the contents.

Other application may be rock detection in wood logs. It could be thickness verification in extruded parts or it could be detection and sorting of diamonds trapped inside kimberlite.
