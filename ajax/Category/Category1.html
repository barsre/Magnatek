Magnatek provides X-ray sources for solutions where dual X-ray energy absorptiometry is ideally suited to measuring organic material. X-rays pass through the entire material to be measured without damaging it in any way. Applications can be in-line process control in the meat plant (fat content) or moisture content in wood chips.

Another application area is contactles parts counting of SMD components on reels.
