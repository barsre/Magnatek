Industrial computed tomography (CT) scanning is an X-ray computer-aided tomographic process, that produce three-dimensional representations of the scanned object both externally and internally. Industrial CT scanning has been used in many areas of industry for internal inspection of components. Applications are within metrology, assembly analysis or flaw detection.
In most applications, microfocus X-ray tubes is needed and Magnatek provides very stable and reliable generators for these purposes.
