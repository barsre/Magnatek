Electronics Circuit Board Inspection

Magnatek provides X-ray sources for solutions where dual X-ray energy absorptiometry is ideally suited to measuring organic material. X-rays pass through the entire material to be measured without damaging it in any way. Applications can be in-line process control in the meat plant or measurement of moisture content in wood chips.

Other industrial applications may be rock detection in wood logs, thickness verification in extruded parts or detection and sorting of diamonds trapped inside kimberlite.
