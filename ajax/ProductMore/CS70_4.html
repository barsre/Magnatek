Max. performance 70 KV/8 mA/280 Watt.

Complete X-ray source consisting of power supply board, with versatile mains input, power control PCB, a microprocessor control, with an RS232 interface, and radiation shielded tube housing, with integrated HT-supply and center grounded X-ray tube.
Focal spot size 0.8 x 0.8 mm and 2 mm inherent filtration. The duty cycle depends on the air cooling system. The CS70 is typical used in measuring/inspection instruments, where a high dose output is required over a short time.
