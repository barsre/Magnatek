Category:

Tag:

Security Screening

Max. performance 60 KV/0.4 mA.

End grounded air cooled X-ray tube 0.3 x 0.5 mm focal spot and 0.125 Ti window. Complete X-ray source consisting of mains transformer, control PCB with analog interface and housing with integrated HT-supply and X-ray tube. The source can be supplied with different kinds of collimators, such as pinhole or fanbeam geometry. The CS60 is typical used for integration in compact measuring/inspection systems. A major application is industrial gauging and filling control systems, where the CS60 is used as replacement for nuclear isotopes. Go to Contact
