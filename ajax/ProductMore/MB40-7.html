Industrial Measuring and Analysis

Maximum performance 40 KV/7 mA/280 Watt.

Continuous operation at 280 Watt only possible with a high efficient air cooling system. End grounded air cooled metal ceramic X-ray tube with 1 x 1 mm focal spot and 1 mm Be window. The X-ray tube is fitted directly to the HT generator by means of a silicon-rubber pad and can be easily changed in the field.

Complete X-ray source integrated into one block with control, HT generation and X-ray tube. Versatile mains input, microprocessor control with RS422 serial and analog interface . The MB40 X-ray source is typical used for integration in high performance measuring and inspection systems.
